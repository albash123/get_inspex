About Us

Getinspex is an innovative inspection management platform developed by Qanzak Global to streamline and modernize inspection processes. It helps businesses optimize workflows, ensure compliance, and reduce inefficiencies through features like real-time GPS tracking, digital checklists, and automated reporting. Designed to enhance accuracy and speed, Getinspex empowers teams to perform inspections smarter and faster. With seamless cloud integration and offline task management, the platform simplifies complex workflows while ensuring compliance with industry standards. Powered by Qanzak Global, Getinspex continues to evolve to meet the growing needs of businesses worldwide
